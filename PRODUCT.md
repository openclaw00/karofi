# Karofi Landing Page

## Register

brand

## Users

Homeowners evaluating a premium water purifier for daily drinking, cooking, and family health. They need to understand product differences quickly, trust the filtration claims, compare models, and reach support without friction.

## Product Purpose

Present Karofi as a credible water purification technology authority and turn product interest into model exploration, consultation requests, and purchases. Success means visitors can identify the right Karofi purifier, understand the technology and certifications, and confidently take the next step.

## Brand Personality

Refined, clinical, authoritative. The experience should feel precise and high quality while remaining calm, approachable, and relevant to a homeowner rather than a laboratory specialist.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic wellness styling, excessive decorative gradients, crowded marketplace layouts, exaggerated health claims, and cold industrial interfaces that obscure practical household benefits.

## Design Principles

- Lead with proof: Pair technical claims with clear standards, specifications, and trust signals.
- Make advanced technology understandable: Translate filtration and monitoring features into concise household benefits.
- Preserve premium restraint: Use deliberate hierarchy, spacing, color, and motion instead of visual noise.
- Support confident comparison: Keep models, pricing, badges, and actions scannable.
- Keep help within reach: Make consultation and service options visible throughout the journey.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.1 AA contrast and interaction practices. Support keyboard navigation, visible focus states, semantic labels, responsive text, and reduced-motion preferences.
